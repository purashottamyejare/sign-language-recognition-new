About This Project

Our Initiative

This is an educational initiative designed to help children learn American Sign Language (ASL) alphabets in a fun and interactive way. We believe in breaking down communication barriers and making sign language learning accessible to everyone, especially young learners.

Why Alphabets Only?

While it's impossible to cover all sign language gestures, we focused specifically on alphabets because they form the foundation of sign language learning. This approach is most helpful for small children who are just starting to learn gestures, making their learning journey easier and more engaging.

Visual Learning

Each detected alphabet is paired with a related image to help children make connections and remember better.

Fun Facts

We've added interesting fun facts for each letter to make learning enjoyable and help children understand better through engaging information.

AI-Powered

Using advanced machine learning and computer vision to provide real-time, accurate alphabet recognition for better learning experience.

Our goal is to contribute to nation building by making sign language education accessible and fun for children, helping them develop communication skills from an early age.
